Mandatory escalation applies

One or more triggers apply, so escalation is required irrespective of the quantitative risk score. At intake, the AI Governance Lead must notify the AI Governance Board immediately (Playbook §3.8, Step 2).

Step 3 of 3

Risk classification Playbook §4.4 and §4.5

Impact is scored across five dimensions and takes the highest, recognising that a single severe impact is enough to make a system high risk (§4.4.4). Inherent risk is likelihood multiplied by impact; residual risk applies the control effectiveness factor (§4.4.2 and §4.4.3).
